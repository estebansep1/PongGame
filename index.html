Ready to Rally?

Step into the retro arena of Pong, where quick reflexes reign supreme. Here's how to handle your paddle:

  • On Computer: Player 1, press 'W' to climb and 'S' to slide down. Player 2, use the Up and Down Arrow keys to navigate your paddle.
  • On Mobile: Simply swipe up or down on your side of the screen to move your paddle. No buttons needed!
